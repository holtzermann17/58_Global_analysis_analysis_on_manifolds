general Stokes theorem


Let M be an oriented r-dimensional differentiable manifold with a piecewise differentiable boundary ∂⁡M. Further, let ∂⁡M have the orientation induced by M. If ω is an (r-1)-form on M with compact support, whose components have continuous first partial derivatives in any coordinate chart, then

∫Mdω=∫∂⁡Mω.
